Embedding video in web page

tag : video -> allows us to embad video directly

  • src: soutce of the video file
  • controls : adds video controls like play , pause , volume
  • width : width of the video player
  • height : height of the video player
  • autoplay : video starts playing as soon as it is loaded
  • loop : video will start over again when finished
  • muted : video will be muted by default
  • Poster: an image to display before the video is palyed
